As of November 9, 2023, **Elon Musk** is the richest person in the world, with a net worth of **$217 billion**. He is the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, and owns a significant stake in Twitter.

Musk's wealth has fluctuated in recent months, but he has remained at the top of the Forbes Billionaires list for most of 2023. His fortune is largely tied to the performance of Tesla, which is the world's most valuable automaker.

Other billionaires in the top 10 include Bernard Arnault, Jeff Bezos, Bill Gates, and Warren Buffett.
